MULTICULTURALISM

While words like “multiculturalism” and “diversity” are hot in the world of early childhood education, it is surprising to learn that just 10% of children’s books in the past two decades have contained any multicultural content at all. Author Nicole Antoinette is proudly standing out like Rudolph’s red nose to put this vital societal issue at the top of every child’s list.
