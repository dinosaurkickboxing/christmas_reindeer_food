Social Causes

Adoption and Foster Awareness

The two main characters are adopted. The plan is to use the books to visit Children Shelters throughout the world as a service project by donating books, reading, fundraising, activities, etc.

Literacy

The plan is to use the books to visit schools throughout the world as a service project by donating books, reading, fundraising, activities, etc.

Multiculturalism

The Christmas Reindeer Food book series supports multiculturalism. It is written by a Black American, edited by a White American, illustrated by a Spainard from Seville, Spain, produced by a Chinese American immigrant, the two main characters are bi-racial, and the dog Zoey is a mixed breed dog.

Nicole Antoinette

As a book publisher and author, Nicole Antoinette owns and manages Nicole Antoinette Consulting d.b.a. Nicole Antoinette Publishing and Faith Books & MORE Publishing. Book buyers know her for leading efforts to publish work as 'A Catalyst for Change.’ She believes writers have an obligation to preserve their thoughts, experiences, creativity, and most importantly, their faith in print, as well as through other forms of media.
